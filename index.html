About The Event

Empowering The Next Generation Of AI Innovators

The AIpreneurs Global Challenge 2025 is an international initiative designed to identify, nurture, and accelerate groundbreaking artificial intelligence startups and solutions. Our mission is to foster a global community of AI entrepreneurs who are committed to solving real-world problems through intelligent technology.

With participants from over 150 countries, innovative solutions addressing healthcare, education, sustainability, and finance, the challenge provides a platform for entrepreneurs to gain recognition, secure funding, and establish partnerships with leading organizations.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility criteria for AIpreneurs Global Challenge 2025

For Startups

  • Registered AI startup or technology company
  • Operating for less than 5 years
  • Team of at least 2 members
  • Viable AI solution with market potential
  • Commitment to the entire challenge duration

For Individual Innovators

  • AI researcher or developer
  • Innovative idea or prototype
  • 18 years or older
  • Ability to form or join a team
  • Commitment to commercialize the idea
