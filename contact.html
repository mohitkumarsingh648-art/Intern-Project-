Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline is March 31, 2025. Selected participants will be announced by April 30, 2025.

Is there a registration fee?

No, there is no registration fee. Applications are completely free, and selected participants receive complimentary access to all program resources.

Can individual innovators apply?

Yes, individual innovators can apply. They must be 18 years or older and have a viable AI prototype or idea with a commitment to commercialize it.

What are the requirements for startups?

Startups must be registered companies, operating for less than 5 years, with at least 2 team members and a core focus on AI solutions.

How long is the program?

The full program runs from January 2025 to August 2025, approximately 8 months with various stages.

What is the prize pool?

The total prize pool is USD 500,000+, with first place receiving USD 150,000, second place USD 100,000, and third place USD 75,000.
