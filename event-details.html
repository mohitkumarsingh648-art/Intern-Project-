Event Overview

Key information about the AIpreneurs Global Challenge 2025

Event Duration

January 2025 - August 2025

Eight month global competition with multiple stages and intensive acceleration period.

Registration Fee

Free to Apply

No registration fees for applicants. Selected participants will receive complimentary access to all program resources and events.

Prize Pool

USD 500,000+

Substantial prizes, grants, and investment opportunities distributed among winning startups and innovators.

Participants Expected

500+ Startups

Startups and innovators from 150+ countries competing across multiple categories.

Challenge Stages

Multi-phase progression through AIpreneurs Global Challenge 2025

January 15 - March 31, 2025
Stage 1: Application & Screening
Interested startups and innovators submit applications online. Our selection committee reviews applications based on innovation, market potential, team quality, and alignment with challenge goals.
April 1 - April 30, 2025
Stage 2: Participant Selection
Top 100 startups selected through rigorous evaluation. Selected teams announce publicly. Orientation program and onboarding process begins.
May 1 - May 31, 2025
Stage 3: Mentorship Program
Participants paired with industry mentors. Weekly mentorship sessions, expert talks, and workshops on business development, technology, and fundraising.
June 1 - June 15, 2025
Stage 4: Bootcamp Intensive
Two-week intensive acceleration program. Immersive workshops, networking sessions, investor presentations, and product development sprints.
July 1 - July 15, 2025
Stage 5: Pitching Round
Top 50 teams pitch to investor panel. Presentations to venture capital firms, angel investors, corporate innovation teams, and media representatives.
August 1 - August 30, 2025
Stage 6: Grand Finale & Awards
Final competition with top 10 teams. Grand ceremony with award presentation, winner announcement, and partnership opportunities.

Eligibility Requirements

Criteria for AIpreneurs Global Challenge participation

Startup Requirements

  • + Must be legally registered as a company or organization
  • + Operating for less than 5 years from date of establishment
  • + Core focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • + Minimum team of 2 full-time members
  • + Viable and scalable product or solution
  • + Clear market opportunity and commercialization strategy

Individual Innovator Requirements

  • + Must be 18 years of age or older
  • + Demonstrated expertise in AI, machine learning, or related field
  • + Existing prototype or proof of concept
  • + Ability to form or join a founding team
  • + Commitment to commercialize the innovation
  • + Willingness to complete full challenge duration

Judging Criteria

How AIpreneurs evaluates startups and innovations

Innovation & Technology

Evaluation of technological advancement, uniqueness of AI approach, and novelty of solution in the market.

Market Potential

Assessment of addressable market size, growth opportunity, and commercial viability of the solution.

Team Quality

Evaluation of founding team's experience, expertise, complementary skills, and execution capability.

Scalability

Assessment of the solution's ability to scale, business model sustainability, and path to profitability.

Impact & Ethics

Evaluation of positive societal impact, ethical considerations, and alignment with sustainable development goals.

Execution & Traction

Assessment of demonstrated progress, customer traction, partnerships, and proof of concept validation.
